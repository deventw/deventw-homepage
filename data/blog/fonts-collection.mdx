## Fonts

- [DM Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/DM+Sans)
  - A clean and modern font with great readability, ideal for digital applications.

- [Murecho](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Murecho)
  - A modern font with high readability and minimal default feel. Designed by Neil Summerour.

- [IBM Plex Sans JP](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/IBM+Plex+Sans+JP)
  - IBM's corporate font, known for its clarity and neutrality.

- [BIZ UDPGothic](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/BIZ+UDPGothic)
  - A free UD font by Morisawa, offering excellent readability for business documents.

Explore these fonts for their unique styles and applications in design projects.
