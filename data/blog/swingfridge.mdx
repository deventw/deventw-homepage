# Swing Fridge iOS App

_Title_: Swing Fridge

_Development Team_: Shayne, Deven, Weilei Xu, Jason, Janson,

_Subject_: Food Tracking Application

_Source_: [Swing Fridge Team Page](https://bento.me/swingfridge)

## Overview

Swing Fridge is an intuitive app designed to help users manage their refrigerator contents efficiently. It provides multiple methods to record and track food items, helping to maintain freshness and minimize waste.

## Development Highlights

- **Technology Stack:** Developed using Swift and SwiftUI, ensuring a modern and responsive design.
- **User Experience:** Close collaboration with designers to create an intuitive and seamless interface.
- **Core Features:**
  - Efficient food tracking with reminders for expiration dates.
  - Customizable categories for different food types.
  - Easy input methods, including NFC scanning.
- **Quality Assurance:** Rigorous testing and debugging to ensure reliability and stability.
- **Collaboration:** Regular feedback loops with users to enhance features and resolve issues.
